🚀 Excited to share my latest project: Qlinic – Clinic Queue Management System 🏥



One of the common problems in clinics is inefficient queue management, often leading to long waiting times and poor patient experience.

To address this, I built Qlinic, a web-based system designed to make queue management more organized and efficient.



💡 Key Features:

-Real-time queue tracking with clear visual feedback

-Smart waiting time estimation

-Role-based dashboards (Admin & Patient)

-Simple and user-friendly interface



🛠 Tech Stack:

-Laravel (Backend)

-Blade & modern UI styling



This project helped me improve not only my backend logic but also how I design systems based on real user needs.



Still open for improvements, and I’d love to hear your feedback! 👇
